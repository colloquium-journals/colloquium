## Platform Overview

Colloquium is designed to make academic publishing transparent and collaborative. Our platform supports:

- **Open Review Process**: Transparent peer review conversations
- **Bot-Assisted Quality Control**: Automated checks and assistance
- **Flexible Publishing**: Multiple formats and submission types
- **Community Building**: Author-reviewer-editor collaboration
