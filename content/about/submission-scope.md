# Submission Scope & Guidelines

Colloquium welcomes high-quality research submissions across a broad range of academic disciplines. Our interdisciplinary approach encourages innovative work that bridges traditional field boundaries.

## What We Publish

### Research Articles
- Original research with novel findings
- Comprehensive studies with significant contributions to the field
- Methodological innovations and their applications

### Review Articles
- Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
- Critical reviews of emerging fields
- Comprehensive surveys of established topics

### Short Communications
- Preliminary findings of ongoing research
- Technical notes and methodological advances
- Comments and responses to published articles

### Data Papers
- Well-documented datasets with clear reusability potential
- Negative results and null findings
- Replication studies

## Scope Areas

While we welcome submissions from all academic disciplines, we particularly encourage research in:

- **Computer Science & Technology**
- **Life Sciences & Medicine**
- **Physical Sciences & Engineering**
- **Environmental & Earth Sciences**
- **Social Sciences & Humanities**
- **Interdisciplinary Studies**

## Submission Requirements

### Manuscript Preparation

1. **Format**: Submit manuscripts in Markdown format for optimal processing
2. **Length**: No strict limits, but typical ranges:
   - Research articles: 3,000-8,000 words
   - Reviews: 4,000-10,000 words
   - Short communications: 1,000-3,000 words

3. **Structure**: Include standard sections appropriate to your field
4. **References**: Use consistent citation format (we support multiple styles)

### Required Elements

- **Abstract**: Clear, concise summary (150-300 words)
- **Keywords**: 3-8 relevant keywords
- **Author Information**: Complete ORCID IDs strongly encouraged
- **Funding Information**: All funding sources must be declared
- **Data Availability**: Statement on data accessibility

### File Submission

- **Manuscript**: Primary document in Markdown or PDF
- **Figures**: High-resolution images in standard formats
- **Supplementary Materials**: Additional data, code, or multimedia
- **Cover Letter**: Brief explanation of significance and fit

## Quality Standards

### Scientific Merit
- Novel contribution to knowledge
- Rigorous methodology
- Appropriate analysis and interpretation
- Clear, well-written presentation

### Ethical Standards
- Original work not previously published
- Proper attribution and citation
- Compliance with research ethics guidelines
- Disclosure of conflicts of interest

### Technical Requirements
- Reproducible methods
- Available data (where applicable)
- Statistical rigor
- Clear figures and tables

## Before You Submit

1. **Review Guidelines**: Ensure your work fits our scope
2. **Check Ethics**: Verify compliance with ethical standards
3. **Prepare Files**: Format according to requirements
4. **Create Account**: Register with your institutional email
5. **ORCID Integration**: Link your ORCID profile for verification

## Submission Process

1. **Initial Submission**: Upload through our web platform
2. **Editorial Screening**: Quick review for scope and quality
3. **Peer Review**: Transparent, conversational review process
4. **Revision**: Collaborative improvement with reviewers
5. **Decision**: Accept, revise, or decline with detailed feedback
6. **Publication**: Immediate open access upon acceptance
