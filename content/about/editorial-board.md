# Editorial Board

Our editorial board consists of distinguished researchers and scholars who volunteer their time and expertise to maintain the highest standards of academic publishing. Each member brings unique perspectives and deep knowledge in their respective fields.

## Editorial Philosophy

Our editorial team is committed to:

- **Fair and Transparent Review**: Every submission receives impartial, constructive evaluation
- **Timely Processing**: Efficient handling of manuscripts while maintaining quality
- **Author Support**: Helping authors improve their work through constructive feedback
- **Innovation**: Embracing new approaches to academic publishing and peer review
- **Diversity**: Representing diverse backgrounds, institutions, and research areas

## Editorial Process

### Initial Review
All submissions undergo initial editorial screening within 5 business days to assess:
- Fit with journal scope
- Technical quality and completeness
- Ethical compliance
- Novelty and significance

### Peer Review Assignment
For manuscripts that pass initial screening:
- Assignment to appropriate editorial board member
- Selection of qualified reviewers
- Coordination of the review process
- Oversight of reviewer discussions

### Decision Making
Editorial decisions are based on:
- Scientific merit and rigor
- Novelty and significance of findings
- Quality of presentation
- Reviewer recommendations
- Community interest and impact

## Board Responsibilities

### Editors
- Handle manuscript assignments in their expertise areas
- Recruit and manage peer reviewers
- Make editorial recommendations
- Facilitate author-reviewer dialogue
- Ensure review quality and timeliness

### Editor-in-Chief
- Oversee all editorial operations
- Make final decisions on manuscript acceptance
- Develop journal policies and procedures
- Represent the journal in the academic community
- Ensure ethical standards and best practices

## Expertise Areas

Our editorial board covers a broad range of disciplines:

- **Physical Sciences & Engineering**

## Join Our Editorial Team

We welcome applications from qualified researchers interested in contributing to our editorial mission. We particularly encourage applications from:

- Strong publication record
- Experience in peer review
